About

I'm an Analytics Engineer and builder with over half a decade of experience working at the intersection of data, AI, and operations for one of the world's largest supply chain networks. I design and ship intelligent automation — systems that don't just report on what happened, but detect anomalies, predict risk, and route the right action to the right owner automatically.

My work spans the full stack: SQL and ETL pipelines, Python backends, deep analysis, supply chain models, and APIs, serverless cloud infrastructure, and web front-ends — increasingly powered by LLMs and AI agents that I integrate into real operational workflows.

I'm at my best turning ambiguous business problems into automated, measurable systems: an agent that monitors inventory health every day, a control tower that flags issues in real time, or a cost-to-serve model that uncovers millions in savings. Across two Fortune 500 supply chains (Amazon and Staples), I've shipped 14+ projects and surfaced $4M+ in cost savings.
